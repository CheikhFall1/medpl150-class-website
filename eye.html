Expressive Eye

This eye was designed to express emotion through shape, color, and composition. I focused on exaggerating certain features such as the pupil and surrounding forms to create a strong emotional impact. The use of line and contrast helps guide the viewer’s attention directly to the eye, making it the central focus of the composition. The overall design emphasizes expression and visual clarity while experimenting with form and balance within the artboard.
